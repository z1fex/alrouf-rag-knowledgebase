# Company Profile & Certifications / الملف التعريفي للشركة والشهادات

## About Alrouf Lighting Technology / عن شركة الروف لتقنية الإضاءة

Alrouf Lighting Technology (الروف لتقنية الإضاءة) is a leading Saudi Arabian manufacturer of outdoor lighting solutions, specializing in street lights, floodlights, garden lights, and lighting poles. Founded in 2007, the company has grown to become one of the Kingdom's premier lighting infrastructure providers, serving municipalities, government agencies, and private contractors across the GCC.

شركة الروف لتقنية الإضاءة هي شركة سعودية رائدة في تصنيع حلول الإضاءة الخارجية، متخصصة في إضاءة الشوارع والكشافات وإضاءة الحدائق وأعمدة الإنارة. تأسست الشركة عام 2007، ونمت لتصبح واحدة من أبرز مزودي البنية التحتية للإضاءة في المملكة، وتخدم البلديات والجهات الحكومية والمقاولين من القطاع الخاص في دول مجلس التعاون الخليجي.

## Company History & Milestones / تاريخ الشركة والإنجازات

**2007** — Founded in Riyadh with a focus on importing and distributing lighting products.

**2010** — Established the first production line for LED street lights at Riyadh Industrial City.

**2012** — Achieved ISO 9001:2008 certification for quality management systems.

**2014** — Secured the first major municipal contract: street lighting for Al-Diriyah Gate Development Project.

**تأسست عام 2007** في الرياض مع التركيز على استيراد وتوزيع منتجات الإضاءة.

**2015** — Expanded factory to 12,000 m². Launched the ALR-SL series.

**2016** — Achieved ISO 14001:2015 certification for environmental management.

**2017** — Won the contract for highway lighting on the Riyadh-Dammam expressway (3,200 luminaires).

**2018** — Launched the ALR-FL floodlight series. Opened service center in Jeddah.

**2019** — Achieved SASO Quality Mark for all street lighting products. Exceeded 50,000 installed luminaires.

**2020** — Opened service center in Dammam. Introduced smart lighting capabilities (DALI/NB-IoT).

**2021** — Awarded lighting contract for NEOM Bay Phase 1.

**2022** — Launched the ALR-GL garden light series. Achieved ISO 45001:2018 for occupational health and safety.

**2023** — Surpassed 100,000 installed luminaires across the Kingdom. Established R&D center in Riyadh.

**2024** — Introduction of solar-hybrid street lighting solutions. Partnership with Saudi smart city initiatives under Vision 2030.

## Manufacturing Facilities / مرافق التصنيع

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Riyadh Industrial City includes:

- **Die-casting workshop**: 4 automated die-casting machines (160T-800T) for aluminum housing production
- **Powder coating line**: Automated electrostatic powder coating with 72-hour salt spray tested finishes
- **LED assembly line**: Semi-automated SMT assembly for LED modules with AOI inspection
- **Testing laboratory**: Accredited photometric laboratory with integrating sphere (2m diameter), thermal chamber (-40°C to +80°C), IP testing chamber, and surge generator
- **Quality control**: Incoming material inspection, in-process quality gates, 100% functional testing before shipping

مرافق التصنيع الحديثة لدينا في المدينة الصناعية بالرياض تشمل ورشة صب بالقالب وخط طلاء بودرة وخط تجميع LED ومختبر اختبار معتمد ومرافق مراقبة الجودة.

## Vision 2030 Alignment / التوافق مع رؤية 2030

Alrouf Lighting Technology is committed to supporting Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 through:

- **Local Content**: Over 65% local content in our products, supporting Saudization goals
- **Smart Cities**: IoT-enabled lighting solutions for smart city projects (DALI, NB-IoT, LoRaWAN)
- **Sustainability**: LED technology reducing energy consumption by 60-70% compared to traditional HPS lighting
- **Saudi Employment**: 45% Saudi workforce, with dedicated training programs for Saudi engineers and technicians
- **Innovation**: Investment of 3% of annual revenue in R&D for next-generation lighting solutions
