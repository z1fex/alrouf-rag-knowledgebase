# Warranty & After-Sales Policy / سياسة الضمان وخدمة ما بعد البيع

## 1. Warranty Coverage / تغطية الضمان

Alrouf Lighting Technology stands behind the quality and durability of every product we manufacture. Our comprehensive warranty program provides peace of mind for our customers, ensuring long-term performance of all installed luminaires.

تقف شركة الروف لتقنية الإضاءة خلف جودة ومتانة كل منتج نصنعه. يوفر برنامج الضمان الشامل لدينا راحة البال لعملائنا، مما يضمن الأداء طويل الأمد لجميع وحدات الإضاءة المثبتة.

### 1.1 Warranty Periods / فترات الضمان

| Component | Warranty Period | Coverage |
|---|---|---|
| LED Module | 5 years | Replacement of LED module if luminous flux drops below L70 within warranty period |
| LED Driver | 5 years | Full replacement of driver unit |
| Surge Protection Module | 3 years | Replacement after documented surge event |
| Housing & Structure | 10 years | Structural integrity, corrosion resistance, powder coating |
| Lens & Gaskets | 3 years | Replacement if IP rating is compromised due to material defect |
| Mounting Hardware | 10 years | Stainless steel bolts, brackets, and arms against corrosion |

### فترات الضمان بالتفصيل

توفر شركة الروف ضماناً لمدة 5 سنوات على وحدات LED والسائقات (الدرايفر)، و10 سنوات على الهيكل والبنية المعدنية. يغطي الضمان عيوب التصنيع وانخفاض أداء الإضاءة عن المستوى المحدد (L70) خلال فترة الضمان.

### 1.2 Warranty Conditions / شروط الضمان

The warranty is valid under the following conditions:

1. **Professional Installation**: The product must be installed by a qualified electrician following the Alrouf Installation & Maintenance Guide (DOC-002).
2. **Proper Power Supply**: Input voltage must be within the specified range (220-240V AC, 50Hz). Voltage fluctuations exceeding ±10% void the electrical component warranty.
3. **Maintenance Compliance**: Regular maintenance must be performed as per the recommended schedule (minimum: annual inspection and bi-annual cleaning).
4. **Original Components**: Only genuine Alrouf replacement parts may be used. Use of third-party components voids the warranty.
5. **Registration**: The product must be registered within 30 days of installation through our warranty portal or service centers.

### 1.3 Warranty Exclusions / استثناءات الضمان

The warranty does NOT cover:

- Damage caused by improper installation or non-compliance with the installation guide
- Damage from power surges exceeding the rated protection level (10kV/20kV depending on model)
- Physical damage from vehicle collision, vandalism, or acts of war
- Natural disasters: earthquakes, floods, or lightning strikes (direct hit)
- Normal wear and tear of consumable items (gaskets after 3 years, lens surface degradation)
- Products modified or tampered with without written authorization from Alrouf
- Damage from operation outside specified environmental conditions (-20°C to +55°C)

لا يغطي الضمان الأضرار الناتجة عن التركيب غير الصحيح أو زيادة الجهد الكهربائي أو الأضرار المادية أو الكوارث الطبيعية أو التعديل غير المصرح به.

## 2. Warranty Claim Process / عملية المطالبة بالضمان

### Step 1: Report the Issue / الإبلاغ عن المشكلة

Contact our after-sales department through any of the following channels:
- **Phone**: +966 11 265 XXXX (Riyadh HQ), available Sunday-Thursday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- **Email**: aftersales@alrouf.com.sa
- **Online Portal**: warranty.alrouf.com.sa
- **Service Centers**: Visit any of our three service centers (Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam)

### Step 2: Provide Documentation / تقديم المستندات

The following information is required:
- Product model and serial number (found on the rating label)
- Installation date and warranty registration number
- Description of the defect with photographs
- Site location and pole/asset number
- Copy of the original purchase order or invoice

### الخطوة 3: التقييم الفني

سيقوم فريقنا الفني بتقييم المطالبة خلال 5 أيام عمل. قد يتطلب الأمر زيارة ميدانية للتحقق من حالة المنتج. سيتم تحديد ما إذا كان العيب مشمولاً بالضمان بناءً على نتائج التقييم.

### Step 3: Technical Assessment

Our technical team will assess the claim within 5 business days. A field visit may be required to verify the product condition. The assessment will determine whether the defect falls under warranty coverage based on the conditions outlined in Section 1.2.

### Step 4: Resolution / الحل

If the claim is approved:
- **Replacement**: Defective component or complete luminaire will be replaced free of charge
- **On-site service**: Installation of replacement will be performed by our field technicians at no additional cost for sites within 100 km of a service center
- **Remote sites**: For sites beyond 100 km, replacement parts will be shipped free of charge; installation labor may be charged at standard rates
- **Timeline**: Standard replacement within 10 business days. Express service (within 48 hours) available for critical infrastructure at additional cost.
