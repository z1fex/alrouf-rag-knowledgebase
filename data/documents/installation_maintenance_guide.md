# Installation & Maintenance Guide / دليل التركيب والصيانة

## 1. Introduction / مقدمة

This guide provides comprehensive installation and maintenance instructions for all Alrouf Lighting Technology outdoor luminaires, including the ALR-SL (Street Light), ALR-FL (Floodlight), and ALR-GL (Garden Light) series. All installation work must be performed by qualified electricians in accordance with Saudi Building Code (SBC) and Saudi Electricity Company (SEC) regulations.

يقدم هذا الدليل تعليمات شاملة للتركيب والصيانة لجميع وحدات الإضاءة الخارجية من شركة الروف لتقنية الإضاءة، بما في ذلك سلسلة ALR-SL (إضاءة الشوارع) و ALR-FL (الكشافات) و ALR-GL (إضاءة الحدائق). يجب أن يتم جميع أعمال التركيب بواسطة كهربائيين مؤهلين وفقاً لكود البناء السعودي ولوائح شركة الكهرباء السعودية.

## 2. Safety Precautions / احتياطات السلامة

⚠️ **تحذير / WARNING**

- Always disconnect power supply before installation or maintenance. / قم دائماً بفصل مصدر الطاقة قبل التركيب أو الصيانة.
- Use appropriate Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) including safety helmet, insulated gloves, and safety harness when working at height. / استخدم معدات الحماية الشخصية المناسبة بما في ذلك خوذة السلامة والقفازات العازلة وحزام الأمان عند العمل على ارتفاع.
- Never open the luminaire while it is connected to the power supply. / لا تفتح وحدة الإضاءة أبداً وهي متصلة بمصدر الطاقة.
- Ensure all cable connections are properly waterproofed using the supplied IP-rated cable glands. / تأكد من عزل جميع توصيلات الكابلات بشكل صحيح باستخدام مدخلات الكابلات المقاومة للماء المرفقة.
- Do not install luminaires during rain, sandstorms, or high-wind conditions (>40 km/h). / لا تقم بتركيب وحدات الإضاءة أثناء المطر أو العواصف الرملية أو ظروف الرياح العالية (أكثر من 40 كم/ساعة).
- Maximum working height for street lights is 12 meters. Use an approved aerial work platform. / الحد الأقصى لارتفاع العمل لإضاءة الشوارع هو 12 متراً. استخدم منصة عمل جوية معتمدة.

## 3. Pre-Installation Requirements / متطلبات ما قبل التركيب

### 3.1 Site Survey

Before installation, conduct a site survey to verify:
- Pole foundation is properly cured (minimum 7 days for concrete foundations)
- Pole height matches the luminaire specification (see table below)
- Underground cable routing is complete and cable pull-through is verified
- No obstructions within the beam angle coverage area
- GPS coordinates are recorded for asset management

### 3.2 Recommended Mounting Heights / ارتفاعات التركيب الموصى بها

| Product | Recommended Height | Road Width | Spacing |
|---|---|---|---|
| ALR-SL-60W | 6-8 m | Up to 8 m | 25-30 m |
| ALR-SL-90W | 8-10 m | Up to 12 m | 30-35 m |
| ALR-SL-120W | 10-12 m | Up to 15 m | 35-40 m |
| ALR-SL-150W | 10-12 m | Up to 18 m | 35-45 m |
| ALR-FL-200W | 8-15 m | Area lighting | Per photometric design |
| ALR-FL-400W | 15-25 m | Area lighting | Per photometric design |
| ALR-GL-30W | 3-4 m | Pathway | 10-12 m |
| ALR-GL-60W | 4-5 m | Garden main path | 12-15 m |

### 3.3 Cable Requirements / متطلبات الكابلات

- Main supply cable: NYY 4×10mm² (for circuits up to 30 luminaires)
- Branch cable to each luminaire: NYY 3×2.5mm²
- Grounding: Dedicated earth conductor, resistance ≤ 4Ω
- Cable entry: Use PG13.5 cable glands (supplied with each luminaire)
- All underground cables must be in HDPE conduit at minimum 600mm depth

## 4. Installation Procedures / إجراءات التركيب

### 4.1 Street Light Installation (ALR-SL Series)

**Step 1: Prepare the mounting arm**
Attach the mounting arm bracket to the pole top using the supplied M12 stainless steel bolts. Torque to 45 Nm.

**Step 2: Connect the wiring**
Thread the supply cable through the pole and arm. Connect wires to the terminal block inside the luminaire:
- Brown wire → L (Live)
- Blue wire → N (Neutral)
- Green/Yellow wire → PE (Earth)

**Step 3: Mount the luminaire**
Slide the luminaire onto the arm spigot (Ø60mm). Secure with the two M8 locking bolts. Adjust the tilt angle as required (0° to +15° for ALR-SL-90W and above).

**الخطوة 4: ضبط زاوية الميل**
اضبط زاوية الميل وفقاً لتصميم الإضاءة المعتمد. يتم تثبيت الزاوية باستخدام مسامير القفل الجانبية. تأكد من إحكام الربط لمنع تحرك وحدة الإضاءة بفعل الرياح.

**Step 5: Test and commission**
Power on and verify:
- Luminaire illuminates correctly
- No flickering or buzzing
- Surge protector LED indicator is green
- Record installation date, pole number, and GPS coordinates

### 4.2 Floodlight Installation (ALR-FL Series)

Install the U-bracket to the mounting structure using M16 bolts (4x). Adjust the floodlight aiming angle using the graduated scale on the bracket sides. Lock in position with the side bolts. Connect wiring as per the terminal diagram on the driver housing.

For pole-top mounting, use the optional ALR-FL-PTA pole-top adapter (ordered separately).

### 4.3 Garden Light Installation (ALR-GL Series)

Garden lights are designed for direct pole-top mounting on Ø60mm decorative poles. Simply slide onto the pole spigot and tighten the set screws. Ensure the base gasket is properly seated to maintain IP65 rating.

## 5. Maintenance Schedule / جدول الصيانة

### 5.1 Routine Maintenance / الصيانة الروتينية

| Task | Frequency | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Visual inspection | Every 3 months | Check for physical damage, corrosion, discoloration |
| Cleaning | Every 6 months | Clean lens with soft cloth and mild soap. Remove sand/dust accumulation |
| Electrical check | Every 12 months | Verify cable connections, check surge protector status, measure current draw |
| Structural check | Every 12 months | Inspect mounting bolts, check for arm fatigue, verify tilt angle |
| Full service | Every 24 months | Complete teardown, gasket replacement, driver inspection, re-torque all bolts |

### 5.2 Cleaning Procedures in Sandy Environments / إجراءات التنظيف في البيئات الرملية

In Saudi Arabia's desert climate, sand and dust accumulation is the primary maintenance concern. Sand buildup on the lens surface can reduce light output by up to 30%.

في مناخ المملكة العربية السعودية الصحراوي، يعتبر تراكم الرمال والغبار الشاغل الرئيسي للصيانة. يمكن أن يقلل تراكم الرمال على سطح العدسة من خرج الضوء بنسبة تصل إلى 30٪.

**Recommended cleaning method:**
1. Use compressed air to remove loose sand particles
2. Wipe lens surface with a damp microfiber cloth
3. For stubborn deposits, use isopropyl alcohol (IPA) solution
4. Do NOT use abrasive materials or pressure washers
5. Inspect gaskets for sand ingress during cleaning

## 6. Troubleshooting / استكشاف الأعطال وإصلاحها

| Symptom | Possible Cause | Solution |
|---|---|---|
| Luminaire does not turn on | No power supply | Check circuit breaker and supply cable continuity |
| Luminaire does not turn on | Driver failure | Replace the LED driver (see spare parts list) |
| Flickering | Loose connection | Re-tighten terminal block connections |
| Flickering | Driver end-of-life | Replace LED driver |
| Reduced light output | Dirty lens | Clean as per Section 5.2 |
| Reduced light output | LED degradation | Normal after 50,000+ hours; plan replacement |
| Water ingress | Damaged gasket | Replace gasket, re-seal cable gland |
| Surge protector LED red | Surge event occurred | Replace surge protection module (SPM) |
| Buzzing noise | Driver vibration | Tighten driver mounting screws; replace if persists |

### Spare Parts / قطع الغيار

All spare parts are available through Alrouf service centers. Common replacement items:
- LED driver module (model-specific)
- Surge Protection Module (SPM-10kV or SPM-20kV)
- Lens gasket kit
- Cable gland set (PG13.5)
- Mounting bolt kit (stainless steel)

Spare parts can be ordered through our service centers in Riyadh, Jeddah, and Dammam. Typical delivery time is 3-5 business days within Saudi Arabia.

يمكن طلب قطع الغيار من خلال مراكز الخدمة لدينا في الرياض وجدة والدمام. مدة التسليم المعتادة هي 3-5 أيام عمل داخل المملكة العربية السعودية.
